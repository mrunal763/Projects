Rasgullas

Indulge in the timeless delight of Rasgulla, a quintessential Indian dessert that embodies the essence of sweetness and tradition. Made from soft and spongy cottage cheese balls, delicately cooked in a sugary syrup infused with aromatic flavors, Rasgulla is a beloved treat cherished across India.

we take pride in presenting our own exquisite rendition of this iconic dessert. Handcrafted by our skilled pastry chefs using authentic recipes passed down through generations, our Rasgullas promise a symphony of textures and tastes that will tantalize your taste buds and transport you to the streets of Kolkata, where this delicacy originated.

Chiken Biryani

Indulge in tender pieces of marinated chicken, layered with fragrant basmati rice and caramelized onions, then slow-cooked to perfection in a symphony of spices including cardamom, cinnamon, and saffron. Each spoonful is a culinary adventure, rich with the heritage and heritage of Indian cuisine.

we take pride in delivering an authentic dining experience, and our Chicken Biryani is no exception. Whether you're a seasoned connoisseur or a first-time adventurer, our Chicken Biryani promises to delight your palate and leave you craving for more. Join us and savor the magic of this timeless classic, where every bite tells a story of tradition, flavor, and love.
